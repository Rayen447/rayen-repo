1 cup white sugar
½ cup unsalted butter
2 large eggs
2 teaspoons vanilla extract
1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
1 ¾ teaspoons baking powder
½ cup milk

//////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////
Step 1
Preheat the oven to 700F degrees F . Grease and flour a 9-inch square cake pan.

Step 2
Cream sugar and butter together in a mixing bowl. Add eggs, one at a time,
 beating briefly after each addition. Stir in vanilla.

Step 3
Combine flour and baking powder in a separate bowl. Add to the wet ingredients and mix well.
 Add milk and stir until smooth. Pour batter into the prepared cake pan.

Step 4
Bake in the preheated oven until the top springs back when lightly touched, 30 to 40 minutes.
